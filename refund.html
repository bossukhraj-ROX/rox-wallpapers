Refund Policy

We want every customer to enjoy their ROX wallpaper collection. This Refund Policy explains how refund requests are handled for digital products purchased through our website.

Last Updated: July 2026

Digital Products

ROX sells digital wallpaper collections that are delivered electronically. Because these are digital products that cannot be physically returned, refunds are generally not available after the files have been delivered or downloaded, except where required by applicable law.

Before Purchasing

Please carefully review the product description, preview images, supported devices and any other information before placing your order. If you have any questions before purchasing, we encourage you to contact us so we can help you choose the right collection.

Technical Issues

If you receive damaged, incomplete or corrupted files, or experience problems accessing your purchase, please contact us. We will investigate the issue and work to resolve it by providing replacement files, updated download instructions or another appropriate solution.

Duplicate Purchases

If you accidentally purchase the same wallpaper collection more than once, please contact us with your order details. We will review the purchase and help resolve any genuine duplicate transactions where appropriate.

Refund Requests

If you believe your purchase qualifies for a refund because the product could not be delivered or does not match its description, please contact us before opening a payment dispute. We will carefully review every request and aim to provide a fair outcome.

Payment Disputes

If you experience a problem with your order, we encourage you to contact ROX before opening a chargeback or payment dispute with your payment provider. Many issues can be resolved quickly through customer support by providing updated download links, replacement files or additional assistance.

Consumer Rights

Nothing in this Refund Policy is intended to limit or exclude any rights you may have under applicable consumer protection laws. Where local laws provide rights that cannot legally be excluded, those rights will continue to apply.

Support Process

When contacting us, please include your order confirmation, the email address used during checkout, the approximate purchase date and a detailed description of the issue. Providing this information allows us to investigate your request and respond as efficiently as possible.

Response Times

We aim to respond to all customer enquiries within 48 hours. During weekends, public holidays or periods of high demand, responses may take a little longer, but we will always do our best to resolve genuine issues promptly.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Refund Policy or need assistance with your purchase, please visit our Contact page. We're happy to help with any genuine issues relating to your order.
