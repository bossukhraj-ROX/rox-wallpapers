Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when using the ROX website.

Last Updated: July 2026

Introduction

ROX is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected and used when you browse our website or purchase our digital wallpaper collections.

Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the website, information may include details you choose to provide, such as your email address when contacting support, as well as technical information like your browser type, device information and general website usage data that helps us improve the website.

How We Use Information

Information is used to respond to enquiries, provide customer support, improve the website, maintain security, fulfil digital purchases, and communicate with customers when necessary regarding orders or support requests.

Payments

Payments are securely processed by Stripe. ROX does not store your full payment card details. Please refer to Stripe's Privacy Policy to learn more about how payment information is handled.

Cookies

ROX may use cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience, remember certain preferences, understand how visitors use the website, and help maintain website performance. You can usually control or disable cookies through your browser settings, although some features of the website may not function as intended if cookies are disabled.

Third-Party Services

ROX uses trusted third-party service providers where necessary to operate the website. This includes Stripe for secure payment processing. These providers process information according to their own privacy policies and security standards.

Data Security

Reasonable technical and organisational measures are used to help protect information against unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. While no website can guarantee absolute security, ROX aims to maintain appropriate safeguards to protect customer information.

Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including the right to request access to information held about you, request corrections, request deletion where appropriate, or object to certain types of processing. If you would like to exercise these rights, please contact us using the details provided on the Contact page.

Children's Privacy

ROX is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under the age required by applicable privacy laws. Personal information is not knowingly collected from children. If you believe information relating to a child has been provided, please contact us so appropriate action can be taken.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect improvements to the website, legal requirements, or changes to our services. The latest version will always be published on this page together with the date it was last updated.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please visit our Contact page to get in touch with us.
