Refund Policy

We want every customer to enjoy their ROX wallpapers. This page explains how refunds are handled for digital wallpaper purchases.

Last Updated: July 2026

Digital Products

ROX sells digital wallpaper collections that are delivered electronically. Because these are digital products and cannot be physically returned, refunds are generally not available once the product has been delivered or downloaded, except where required by applicable law.

Before You Purchase

Please review the product description, preview images, supported devices and any other information provided before completing your purchase. If you have questions about a collection before buying, we encourage you to contact us so we can help you make an informed decision.

Technical Issues

If you experience a genuine technical problem, such as receiving corrupted files, incomplete downloads or being unable to access the purchased content, please contact us. We will work with you to resolve the issue by providing replacement files, updated download instructions or other appropriate assistance.

Duplicate Purchases

If you accidentally purchase the same collection more than once, please contact us with your order details. We will review the situation and, where appropriate, help resolve the duplicate purchase.

Refund Requests

If you believe you are entitled to a refund because your purchase could not be delivered, the files are defective, or the product does not match its description, please contact us before initiating a payment dispute. We will review your request as quickly as possible and work with you to find a fair solution.

Payment Disputes

If you experience a problem with your order, we encourage you to contact ROX before opening a chargeback or payment dispute. In many cases, technical issues can be resolved much more quickly by providing updated download links or replacement files.

Consumer Rights

Nothing in this Refund Policy is intended to limit or exclude any rights that you may have under applicable consumer protection laws. Where local law provides rights that cannot legally be excluded, those rights will continue to apply.

Support Process

When contacting us, please include as much information as possible, such as your order confirmation, the email address used during checkout, and a description of the issue you experienced. This helps us investigate your request and respond more efficiently.

Response Times

We aim to acknowledge support requests within 48 hours. Response times may vary during weekends, holidays, or periods of unusually high demand, but we will always aim to resolve genuine issues as promptly as possible.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Refund Policy or need assistance with an order, please visit our Contact page and we'll be happy to help.
