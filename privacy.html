Refund Policy

We want every customer to enjoy their ROX wallpapers. This page explains how refunds are handled for digital wallpaper purchases.

Last Updated: July 2026

Digital Products

ROX sells digital wallpaper collections that are delivered electronically. Because these are digital products and cannot be physically returned, refunds are generally not available once the product has been delivered or downloaded, except where required by applicable law.

Before You Purchase

Please review the product description, preview images, supported devices and any other information provided before completing your purchase. If you have questions about a collection before buying, we encourage you to contact us so we can help you make an informed decision.

Technical Issues

If you experience a genuine technical problem, such as receiving corrupted files, incomplete downloads or being unable to access the purchased content, please contact us. We will work with you to resolve the issue by providing replacement files, updated download instructions or other appropriate assistance.

Duplicate Purchases

If you accidentally purchase the same collection more than once, please contact us with your order details. We will review the situation and, where appropriate, help resolve the duplicate purchase.

Third-Party Services

ROX uses trusted third-party service providers where necessary to operate the website. This includes Stripe for secure payment processing. These providers process information according to their own privacy policies and security standards.

Data Security

Reasonable technical and organisational measures are used to help protect information against unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. While no website can guarantee absolute security, ROX aims to maintain appropriate safeguards to protect customer information.

Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including the right to request access to information held about you, request corrections, request deletion where appropriate, or object to certain types of processing. If you would like to exercise these rights, please contact us using the details provided on our Contact page.

Children's Privacy

ROX is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under the age required by applicable privacy laws. Personal information is not knowingly collected from children. If you believe information relating to a child has been provided, please contact us so appropriate action can be taken.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect improvements to the website, legal requirements, or changes to our services. The latest version will always be available on this page together with the date it was last updated.
